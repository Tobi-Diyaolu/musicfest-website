Festival Lineup

Sade

Soul / Jazz

Legendary Nigerian-British singer Sade Adu is known for her smooth, elegant voice and timeless tracks such as "Smooth Operator" and "No Ordinary Love." Since emerging in the 1980s, her music has blended soul, jazz, and soft rock with an unmistakable coolness.

Across a career spanning decades, Sade's recordings and live performances have been praised for their restraint, musicality and emotional clarity. She has influenced generations of vocalists and continues to be celebrated for the craft of her songwriting and the warmth of her delivery.

Radiohead

Alternative Rock / Experimental

Radiohead are an English band whose inventive approach to songwriting and production has reshaped alternative music. Records such as "OK Computer" and "Kid A" combined striking melodies with experimental textures and political undercurrents.

Over their career they have moved from guitar-driven rock into electronic and avant-garde territories, earning critical acclaim and a devoted fanbase. Their work is notable for its emotional depth, sonic exploration and willingness to take risks.

Dave Brubeck

Jazz

Dave Brubeck is the legendary jazz pianist and composer behind classics such as "Take Five." His cool jazz approach and melodic inventiveness brought improvisation and unusual time signatures into the mainstream.

Across a long career he pushed jazz’s boundaries while maintaining a clear sense of melody and swing. Brubeck's influence extends to musicians across genres who admire his compositional skill and rhythmic daring.

H.E.R

R&B / Soul

H.E.R. is a Grammy-winning American singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist, praised for her emotive vocals and modern R&B sensibility. Her songs pair intimate storytelling with lush harmonies.

Since her rise, H.E.R. has been recognized both for songwriting craft and for her live performances which combine instrumental skill and deep emotional expression. She continues to shape contemporary soul and R&B.

Tame Impala

Psychedelic / Indie Rock

Tame Impala is Kevin Parker’s project, noted for lush production that blends psychedelic rock with pop and electronic textures. Songs like "Let It Happen" showcase sweeping arrangements and introspective lyricism.

Parker’s studio focus and layered sonic palettes have brought modern psychedelic music into the mainstream, influencing both indie and electronic artists while remaining distinctively melodic.

Frank Ocean

Alternative R&B

Frank Ocean is acclaimed for his intimate, genre-blurring approach to songwriting. Albums like "Channel Orange" and "Blonde" combine personal narratives with experimental production and sharp melodic sense.

Ocean's emotionally resonant lyrics and unconventional song structures have made him a pivotal figure in 21st-century music, influencing artists across R&B, pop and indie scenes.
