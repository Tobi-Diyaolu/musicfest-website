Join Us on 5th-6th July 2025 at Central Park

MusicFest 2025 is a two-day celebration of live music, bringing together a diverse lineup of world-class performers. From jazz legends to contemporary indie, soulful R&B, and electronic beats, the festival offers an immersive musical experience for fans of all genres. Set in the heart of Central Park, attendees can enjoy both vibrant performances and scenic outdoor spaces designed for relaxation, socialising, and food experiences.

Festival-goers can expect electrifying performances across multiple stages, interactive workshops, art installations, and food stalls featuring cuisines from around the world. MusicFest aims to create a memorable cultural experience that goes beyond the music, celebrating creativity, community, and the joy of live performances.
